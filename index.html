Best Brownies in Town

Alexandra Douglas is the new Owner/Operator of A Fresh Start Gluten Free Inc., a new Gluten Free Bakery serving Ottawa’s Gluten Free and Celiac Community. A Fresh Start has taken over shop from FUK Flour, and will continue to serve up FUK Flour treats, breads, and great service while growing her brand new business with a product line of her own.
